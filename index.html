The Inspiration

Donut Maker, a game for anyone who has a strong love of delicious donuts but who are unfortunately plagued by the very real and very dangerous allergy to exercise, was inspired by the original Cookie Clicker Game.

Just like Cookie Clicker, click away and enjoy yourself! The more donuts you make the more perks you get! MUCH better than real life.

Inspiration

About Donut Clicker

You can earn auto clickers and multipliers which will battle right along side you to collect as many delicious donuts as you could only dream of.
But Fear Not!! If someone should come along, like a personal trainer for example, all you need do is click the ever handy reset button which will erase your donut binge without judgement. Leave all guilt and hesitation at the door of Crys' Donut Shop, dive in and most importantly, ENJOY YOURSELF!
